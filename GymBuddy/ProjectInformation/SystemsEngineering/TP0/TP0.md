## **Description of the System**
### **Purpose of GymBuddy**
The **GymBuddy** mobile app is designed for gym enthusiasts facing the challenge of **forgetting workouts** or struggling to **choose exercises**. The app provides:  
- **Pre-made workout plans** with recommended exercises.
- **Custom workout creation** by selecting exercises from a provided list.
- **Exercise demonstrations** for proper execution guidance.
- **Calendar integration** to schedule and track workouts.
- **Workout partner matching** based on location, gym, and schedule preferences.

### **Key Features:**
1. **Workout Planning:**
   - Users can **choose or create** workouts.
   - Each workout includes exercise selection, sets, reps, and weight customization.
2. **Exercise Guidance:**
   - Tutorials and demonstrations for proper form.
3. **Calendar & Scheduling:**
   - Users can set workout reminders and track progress.
4. **Partner Matching:**
   - Find gym partners based on availability and location.
5. **User-Friendly Experience:**
   - Intuitive UI and seamless navigation for an enhanced gym experience.

The app is designed to **enhance gym motivation, organization, and efficiency** for users looking to **structure and improve** their workout routines.
