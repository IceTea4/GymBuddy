## HIIT
- **Treadmill:** 30sec sprint; 20sec walk/jog. *15 cycles*
- **Bicycle:** 20sec all out; 10sec cruise. *24 cycles*
- **Stairs:** 12lv single step 1min; 15lv double step 2min. *5 cycles*
- **Heavy Rope:** waves, slams, throws, spirals, whips; 30sec intervals; 15sec rest between intervals. *10 sets*
- **Anywhere:** 30sec sprint; 15 burpees; 30 high knees; 25sec rest. *10 cycles*
- **Park Bench:** 30sec sprint; 15 single leg step-ups / 15 squat jumps; 15 tricep dips; 45sec rest. *10 cycles*
- **Indoor:** 15 lunge jumps / 15 push-ups; 25 squats; 20 chair dips; 40 mountain climbers; 35sec rest. *10 cycles*
- **Track:**
  - 2x sprint 100m and jog the rest of the lap
  - 2x sprint 200m and jog the rest of the lap
  - 2x side shuffle 100m facing each side
  - Sprint 100m and jog the rest of the lap
  - Jog 1 lap *1 cycle*
