# Style Guide for Our App

## **Typography**
- Font: **Poppins**

## **Color Palette**
- **White**: `#FFFFFF`
- **Black**: `#1D1D1B`
- **Blue**: `#2F4697`

## **Text Colors**
- **Default text color**: Black (`#1D1D1B`)
- **Text on non-white backgrounds**: White (`#FFFFFF`)

## **Section Backgrounds**
- **Menu section**: Blue (`#2F4697`)
- **Highest section**: Black (`#1D1D1B`)
- **Body & other sections**: White (`#FFFFFF`)

## **Buttons**
- All buttons should be **black** (`#1D1D1B`)

## **Dividers**
- Black lines should separate sections with a **white** background.

## **Flexibility**
These styles are preliminary and can be modified as needed during development.
